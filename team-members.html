Leadership Team

Meet our distinguished leaders who guide the Lumbini Development Trust

Rt. Honorable President

Patron

The President of Nepal serves as the Patron of Lumbini Development Trust, providing the highest level of support and guidance for the preservation and development of this sacred site. Under the constitutional framework, the President ensures that Lumbini's development aligns with national heritage preservation goals.

Office: Office of the President

Location: Sheetal Niwas, Kathmandu

Established: Constitutional Position

Honorable Prime Minister

Chairman

The Prime Minister of Nepal chairs the Lumbini Development Trust, ensuring that the development of Lumbini aligns with national priorities and international commitments. The Chairman oversees strategic planning, policy implementation, and international cooperation for Lumbini's development as a world peace city.

Office: Office of the Prime Minister

Location: Singha Durbar, Kathmandu

Authority: Executive Head of Government

Hon. Minister

Vice Chairman

The Minister of Culture, Tourism and Civil Aviation serves as Vice Chairman, overseeing the day-to-day operations and strategic development of Lumbini. The Vice Chairman coordinates between various ministries, international organizations, and stakeholders to ensure comprehensive development of the sacred site.

Ministry: Culture, Tourism and Civil Aviation

Location: Singha Durbar, Kathmandu

Portfolio: Tourism & Cultural Heritage
