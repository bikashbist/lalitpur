Discover the Kathmandu Valley

Kathmandu valley is the cultural, commercial and political center of Nepal. Kathmandu valley is situated at an altitude of approximately 1,400 meters above sea level and is surrounded by four major hills: Shivapuri, Phulchoki, Nagarjun and Chandragiri.

The valley consists of three districts: Kathmandu, Lalitpur and Bhaktapur. These three districts are home to the three medieval kingdoms of Kathmandu, Patan and Bhaktapur respectively.
